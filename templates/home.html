Introduction

BACKGROUND

  • The VTC manpower survey collects information of employees, such as count, income, and education, to formulate training with the government and different stakeholders.
  • The process of retrieving, cleaning, and classifying unstructured job advertisements into Principal Job Titles is ongoing.
  • Currently, the accuracy of the classification is around 46.45%.

BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS

  • A model that can accurately classify job advertisements into Principal Job Titles is needed.
  • The model is expected to generate accurate predictions and classifications of job codes.

PROJECT OBJECTIVES

  • To utilize the knowledge learned in class to identify factors that influence job classifications.
  • To construct a prediction and analytic model to identify job classifications in real situations.
  • To enhance the efficiency of the operation of the Manpower Survey conducted by the HIP of VTC, including data collection, cleaning, modeling, and classification of job advertisements.
